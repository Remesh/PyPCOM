Advanced Examples
=================

PyPCOM is extremely powerful and flexible, but it might not be clear at first
how that power and flexibility can be leveraged. Here you'll find a collection
of structures and approaches that go beyond just having a straightforward page
structure. They're are meant to serve as templates to be pulled from, or just
inspiration.
